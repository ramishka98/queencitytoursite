adventure Activities

Bungy Jumping

Kawarau Bridge Bungy Centre is the birthplace of commercial bungy jumping, and it's still one of the most popular places to do it. You can jump from the Kawarau Bridge, which is 43 meters high, or from the nearby Nevis Swing, which is the highest swing in the world.

Shotover Jet

Shotover Jet boat ride takes you through the Shotover River canyon, one of the most scenic and challenging stretches of water in New Zealand. You'll speed through narrow canyons and over white-water rapids, all while enjoying the stunning views

Ski Carving

Coronet Peak Ski Resort is located just outside of Queenstown and offers stunning views of the Remarkables mountain range. You can ski or snowboard in the winter, or go for a hike or mountain bike ride in the summer.

Bird Watching

Gibbston Valley Canopy zip-line tour takes you through the Gibbston Valley wine region, with stunning views of the vineyards and surrounding mountains. You'll zip across gorges and over rivers, all while getting a bird's-eye view of the valley.
